Research Experience

 Fracture characteristics of rubber concrete were studied using X-ray computed tomography (XCT) technology with image processing algorithms [1, 2, 3, 4, 5].
  • A novel image processing method combined with an in-situ XCT test was developed to track the fracture process of rubberized concrete specimens under loading [1].
  • A sophisticated image processing method was proposed to quantitatively analyze crack morphological features by detecting continuous and integral microcracks accurately from XCT images and effectively removing all the pores and additions with similar gray values in the cracks' final detected images [2].
  • XCT and digital volume correlation (DVC) were adopted to investigate the internal structure and volumetric strain of fractured rubber cement composites[3, 4, 5].
